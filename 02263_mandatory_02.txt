Net
	- Stop
		* Name
		* Capacity (Maximum number of trams allowed to be at a stop at the same time)
	- Connection
		* Capacity (Maximum number of trams allowed to be on the single track at the same time)
		* Minimum driving time (the time it MUST at least take for a tram to drive between the two stops)
		* Minimum headway (the minimum time that MUST pass between two following trains entering or leaving the same track of the conn)

Time Tables
	- Tram
		* Unique name
	
	- Time Table
		- name of tram
		- plan for that tram
			* the name of the stops in the net where the tram will stop
			* for each stop: arrival- and departure time
		
	- Time
		In whole minutes
